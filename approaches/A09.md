## Must

- Named roles with clear ownership boundaries
- Integration step before verify (swarm output must compose)
- Conflict resolution rule documented (who wins on overlap)

## Must not

- Uncoordinated parallel edits to same files without merge plan
- Skipping integration verify

## Review model

Role leads review their surface; one integrator owns final PR.

## Team notes

| Size | Compression |
|------|-------------|
| 1 | Multiple AI roles; human integrates |
| 2–3 | Humans take specialist roles + AI helpers |
| 5 | Natural fit; still need integrator |

## Done when

Role log present; integrated oracle green; metrics recorded.
