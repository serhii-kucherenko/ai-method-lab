htsroute

Offline research demo of the customs form gate: same molecule, different route by bulk vs mixed pellets vs measured-dose tablets. Molecule name is ignored on purpose. Fixture pass counts are not market proof.

Disclaimer. Brokers and existing HS tools already win this commercially. This page is a workflow experiment, not a product pitch. For pantoprazole (PPI) showcase pairs, ordinary MFN duty is often Free / Free on both bulk Chapter 29 and finished heading 3004 — flipping the chapter does not save duty for that family.
Stacked duties out of scope. Letter base MFN (6% / 6.5% ↔ Free) is not the landed-duty story for patented pharma once Section 232 layers apply (proclamation: Annex III from 2026-07-31; other companies from 2026-09-29). Generic pharmaceuticals are not subject to these 232 tariffs at this time — so the acetaminophen / ibuprofen / aspirin teaching pairs are not a 232 savings story either. This demo does not compute 232 / 301 dollars or annex membership — see docs/ideas/htsroute-STACKED-TARIFF-FENCE.md.

Examples teach money honesty and form-gate discipline — not vanity pass counts.
