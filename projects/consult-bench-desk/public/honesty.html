Honesty

Consult Bench Desk is a method-lab experiment inspired by paper 2607.09142 — multimodal real-world scoring against text_only / image_blind / synthetic-chat baselines, with soft simulation only.

It is not a replacement for the authors' MedRealMM benchmark and not a telemedicine consult service. Never brand this desk as a telemedicine product.

Paper: https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.09142v1 (2607.09142). Authors' code: none published.
