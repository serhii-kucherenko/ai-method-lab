Honesty

Memory Risk Desk is a method-lab experiment inspired by paper 2607.11656 — imputation-free calibrated scoring against mean_impute / uncalibrated / single-cohort baselines, with soft simulation only.

It is not a replacement for the authors' transformer and not a clinical diagnostic product. Never brand this desk as a medical device.

Paper: https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.11656v2 (2607.11656). Authors' code: none published.
