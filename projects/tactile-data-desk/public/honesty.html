Honesty

A method-lab product for touch-first chart exploration experiments — soft layered select, confirm, ask agent, and verify on chart with an audit trail.

This is not a replacement for the authors' system. It is not a commercial tactile accessibility product. Never brand as Feelogue. Dot Pad may appear only in Sources / honesty context — never as the product name.

Paper: https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.14588v1 (2607.14588). Authors' code: https://github.com/accessible-data-vis/feelogue.
