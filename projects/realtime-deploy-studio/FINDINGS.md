# FINDINGS — Realtime Deploy Studio

## What worked
- Distinct IA (`/apps` `/deploy` `/readiness`) kept the product off the desk-clone ban.
- Dual-impl goldens locked harnessed vs naive scoring before UI polish.
- Night slate + amber visual direction matched “live deploy signal” without purple-AI defaults.

## What we learned
- Harness lift is the sellable claim: IR + measurement gates beat single-shot under multimodal pressure.
- Readiness as its own page makes TTFO / throughput / sync / correctness visible without stuffing the deploy console.
- Soft-simulation fence must say “not FlashRT brand” and “not a production autoscaler” on honesty and landing.

## Limits
- Scores are plan-quality proxies, not measured GPU latency from B200/MI355X runs.
- No real coding-agent loop — stages simulate the harness workflow.
