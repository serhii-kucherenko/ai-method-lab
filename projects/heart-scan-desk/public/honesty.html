Heart Scan Desk is a method-lab experiment inspired by paper 2607.11287 — unified segmentation+phenotyping scoring against seg_only / pheno_pixels / single-center baselines, with soft simulation only.

It is not a replacement for the authors' foundation model and not a clinical diagnostic product. Never brand this desk as a medical device.

Paper: https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.11287v1 (2607.11287). Authors' code: none published.
