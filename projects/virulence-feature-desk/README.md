# Virulence Feature Desk

Method-lab product sourced from a simple-papers digest.

## Paper

- **Id:** 10.1186/s40168-026-02467-w
- **Title:** SEVA: structural and evolutionary feature integration for predicting virulence factors and antibiotic resistance genes
- **URL:** https://doi.org/10.1186/s40168-026-02467-w
- **Code:** https://github.com/kaiqili2/SEVA

## What we will build

SEVA advances the field of bioinformatics by integrating protein language models with structural and evolutionary features, providing a more accurate and robust method for predicting virulence factors and antibiotic resistance genes than previous tools.
