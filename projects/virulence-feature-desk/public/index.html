Virulence Feature Desk

Structural and evolutionary signals should move risk — not sequence alone.

Organize orgs, projects, and structural+evolutionary feature jobs for method experiments — a lab desk, not the authors’ tool, and not a clinical diagnostic.

Open desk Read honesty

The problem

Sequence-only scoring misses when structure and evolution align on virulence or resistance risk. Teams need a clear compare of integrated features against a flat baseline — with lifecycle and audit that stay honest under concurrent edits.

The product

Virulence Feature Desk is an org workspace for feature jobs: create work, enforce lifecycle, compare sequence-only vs structural+evolutionary scoring, batch sibling transitions, and keep an exportable audit. Inspired by the paper; never a clinical diagnostic.

Why teams use it

  • See when integrated structural+evolutionary fit beats sequence-only.
  • Keep every compare, transition, and reject on an audit trail you can export.
  • Run batch sibling updates without one failure poisoning the rest.
  • Browse dual-implementation goldens so desk math stays checkable.
  • Rotate signed webhooks without leaving the org settings page.
