Drug Discovery Desk

Disease context should change which candidates rise — not a flat unconditioned score.

Organize orgs, projects, and disease-conditioned discovery jobs for method experiments — a lab desk, not the authors’ model, and not a wet-lab or regulatory product.

Open desk Read honesty

The problem

Unconditioned generation treats every indication the same. Teams need a clear compare of disease-aware scoring — where indication tags align with the disease context — against a flat baseline, with lifecycle and audit that survive concurrent edits.

The product

Drug Discovery Desk is an org workspace for discovery jobs: create work, enforce lifecycle, compare unconditioned vs disease-aware scoring, batch sibling transitions, and keep an exportable audit. Inspired by the paper; never a wet-lab or regulatory stand-in.

Why teams use it

  • See when disease-conditioned fit beats a flat unconditioned score.
  • Keep every compare, transition, and reject on an audit trail you can export.
  • Run batch sibling updates without one failure poisoning the rest.
  • Browse dual-implementation goldens so desk math stays checkable.
  • Rotate signed webhooks without leaving the org settings page.

Features

Live capabilities grouped for scanning — not a raw CRUD dump.

Org & jobs

  • Org tenancy and member roles

  • Pagination and search

Workflow

  • Lifecycle draft → queued → running → terminal
  • Illegal transition reject
  • Optimistic version conflict

Compare & evidence

  • Scenario: unconditioned vs disease-aware
  • Audit log and CSV export
  • Goldens browser (≥25 fixtures)
  • Honesty and Sources pages

Integrations

  • Webhook inbound with HMAC and idempotency

  • Rate-limit feedback (429 + Retry-After)
  • Offline try page and tutor guide
