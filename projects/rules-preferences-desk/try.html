Rules Preferences Desk

Offline demo. Compare naive preference-only packing against hard-rule gated preference selection — core claim of this method-lab product.

Honesty: Method experiment inspired by paper 2607.15562. This is not a replacement for the authors' packing checklist system. Authors' code: https://github.com/Official529Tech/rlo-checklist. Not a commercial packing app. Never brand as FlyEnJoy or RLO. Default catalog with capacity 8 often shows naive violations while constrained stays feasible.
