Stage Validate Desk is a method-lab experiment inspired by paper 2607.14568 — stage-gate · long-context · bit-measure scoring against naive intuition baselines, with soft simulation only.

It is not a replacement for the authors' Fermi CUDA engine or commercial inference stacks. Never brand MiniCPM, Fermi, or Tesla C2075 as the product name.
