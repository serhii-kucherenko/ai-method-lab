Chest Xray Desk is a method-lab experiment inspired by paper 2607.09305 — classify→localize→validate scoring against classify_only / localize_no_gate / threshold-alert baselines, with soft simulation only.

It is not a replacement for the authors' Thailand deep learning system and not a clinical diagnostic product. Never brand this desk as a medical device.
