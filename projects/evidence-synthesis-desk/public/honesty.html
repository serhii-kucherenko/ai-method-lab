Honesty

A method-lab product for screened evidence synthesis experiments — eligibility, standardized effect sizes, and random-effects pooling with an audit trail.

This is not a replacement for the authors' system. It is not a commercial evidence-synthesis vendor. Never brand as AutoSynthesis. PRISMA may appear only in Sources / honesty context — never as the product name.
