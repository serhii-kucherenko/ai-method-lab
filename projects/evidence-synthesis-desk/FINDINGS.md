# Findings — Evidence Synthesis Desk

Screened eligibility → Hedges' g → DerSimonian–Laird random-effects (+ I²) vs naive average of reported numbers (skip screening / include ineligible). Dual-impl goldens (≥30 fixtures) agree on A/B. Desk pattern: marketing landing at `/`, org, projects, synthesis jobs, lifecycle, batch, audit, webhooks, honesty. Never branded AutoSynthesis. Authors' code: none published.

## What worked

- Contaminated corpora make screening deltas obvious next to naive averages.
- Rejecting `perfect_homogeneity_cheat` documents what the score is not.
- Keeping harness aliases (`naive` / `pla`) let the org desk pattern stay stable while domain math changed.

## Limits

- Toy corpora, not live literature search or PDF extraction.
- Not a PRISMA workflow product; not clinical decision support.
