# PRODUCT — Thorax Localize Studio

**Buyer:** Chest imaging teams who need disease classification **and** lesion localization on CXR.

**Outcome:** Register CXR exams, review multi-disease findings, inspect lesion maps, run clinical validation, and compare against classify-only baselines.

**Fence:** Method-lab soft scores only — not clinical certification or live PACS. Not branded as Inspectra.

**Paper:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.09305v1 (no authors’ public code)
