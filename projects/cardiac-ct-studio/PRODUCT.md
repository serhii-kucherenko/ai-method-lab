# PRODUCT — Cardiac CT Studio

**Buyer:** Cardiac imaging teams who need HITL annotation + multi-structure segmentation + phenotyping.

**Outcome:** Register CT studies, run expert annotation queues, track segments, review phenotypes, apply augment policies, and compare against auto-only baselines.

**Fence:** Method-lab soft scores only — not clinical certification or live PACS.

**Paper:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.11287v1 (no authors’ public code)
