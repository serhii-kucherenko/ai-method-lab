Itinerary Plan Desk

Offline demo. Compare naive preference-only day schedules against feasibility-first plan / learn / adapt.

Inspired by paper 2607.15552. This is not a replacement for the authors' on-device itinerary system. Code: https://github.com/Official529Tech/pla-itinerary. Not a commercial trip planner. Never brand as FlyEnJoy or PLA. Default catalog with day budget 8 often shows naive window violations while plan/learn/adapt stays feasible.
