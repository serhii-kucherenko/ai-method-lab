# Heart Rhythm Desk — findings

## Sustain

Green on Next.js + Tailwind + shadcn with dual-impl goldens (≥28), UI critical paths on nine pages, and honesty fences.

## Claim

Long-tail-aware rhythm scoring vs majority baseline. Rare-class tag overlap raises the integrated score; majority cheat rejects.

## Honesty

Not a clinical ECG reader. Not a replacement for the authors' model at https://github.com/Open-EXG/AG-SCL-for-Long-Tailed-ECG. Paper: https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.14613v1.

## Design

Shipped against `docs/ideas/heart-rhythm-desk-DESIGN.md` (teal paper atmosphere, Fraunces + Source Sans 3, ECG trace motion).
