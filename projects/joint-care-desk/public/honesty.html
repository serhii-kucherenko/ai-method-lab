Joint Care Desk is a method-lab experiment inspired by paper 2607.12527 — hospital · external · stage scoring against naive parametric policy baselines, with soft simulation only.

It is not a replacement for the authors' OrthoPilot system or commercial inference stacks. Never brand OrthoPilot as the product name.
