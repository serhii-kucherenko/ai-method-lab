# Hypothesis — Filing Penalty Desk

Controllers often forecast late-file / late-pay exposure as a flat percent. Month-by-month bases, same-month file reduction, levy bumps, caps, and minimum floors change the dollars — sometimes materially.

**Falsifiers:** domain experts reject the month-walk; finance still uses a flat late-fee cell after smoke; product collapses to a dual-approver board or statute-code brand; digests claim IRS/CPA replacement.
