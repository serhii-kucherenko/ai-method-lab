Filing Penalty Desk

Offline demo. Month-by-month failure-to-file and failure-to-pay additions with same-month file reduction — not a flat late-fee widget.

Kill A: IRS notices and commercial tax software already assess. This is a cash-forecast / method honesty desk — not an IRS assessment, abatement, or CPA replacement. Toy defaults: $10,000 unpaid one late-file month → failure-to-file $450 and failure-to-pay $50 (combined $500), not a stacked 5.5%.
