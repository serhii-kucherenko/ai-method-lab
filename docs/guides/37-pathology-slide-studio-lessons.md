# Pathology Slide Studio — what we learned

## Story
Computational pathology teams do not need another jobs desk. They need a studio that mirrors how foundation eval actually fragments: vision patches, language-aligned concepts, and slide-level context. Pathology Slide Studio ships that buyer story with cohorts, embed, slides, compare, and audit — without isomorphic `/jobs` `/lifecycle` `/scenario` shells.

## Lessons
1. **IA is the anti-clone.** Distinct primary routes beat noun-swapping a prior studio's panel/predict pages.
2. **Multi-signal vs vision-only is the claim.** Dual-impl goldens prove the math; they do not require shipping ALICE weights or branding.
3. **Honesty early.** Landing and `/honesty` must state non-clinical fence and Sources before sustain email.
4. **Live app gate.** Unit goldens alone are not sustain — `next build` + `next start` smoke must show the display name.
5. **Design tokens matter.** Rose-gray + deep teal with Cormorant / DM Sans keeps the product off purple-AI defaults.
