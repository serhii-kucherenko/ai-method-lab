# Pocket Molecule Desk — lessons

**Product:** Pocket Molecule Desk

**Authors' code:** none published  

## What this desk teaches

1. **Dual-gate must be unique.** Pocket-conditioned + property-aware (affinity **and** developability) is not the same as dual-approver boards or a drug-discovery language-model desk.
2. **Naive baselines are first-class.** Unconditioned / ligand-only, affinity-only, and property-blind pocket fill sit beside the good plan on the scenario page — not as footnotes.
3. **Soft simulation stays honest.** No ConDitar / msPRL / paOPT / CDH branding; no claim of real docking or ADMET assays.
4. **Live app gate matters.** Unit green without `next build` + production GET `/` is not sustain-ready.
5. **Rebrand leftovers fail digests.** Hunt prior desk names (joint-care, clinical EHR, OrthoPilot) after cloning a mature desk.
