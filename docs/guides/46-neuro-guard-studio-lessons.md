# Neuro Guard Studio — lessons

## What worked
- Distinct IIoT IA (`sites` / `sensors` / `plans` / `counterfactuals` / `interventions`) avoided desk and Data World noun-swaps.
- Dual score (neuro-agentic CPI vs reactive threshold) made the “simulate before act” claim testable without shipping live ICS control.
- Intervention status transitions plus CPI projected-vs-baseline deltas matched the buyer story better than a generic compare page alone.

## What to watch
- Soft sims must keep honesty fences loud — buyers will assume “physics injection” means a live plant twin.
- Threshold noise and cascade risk should clearly hurt the reactive path so A beats B on cascade avoided.
- Never brand as the paper’s Neuro-Agentic Control product; keep Sources pointing at the paper only.
