# Ladder Bomb Studio — lessons

## What worked
- Distinct OT IA (`plants` / `programs` / `scans` / `triggers` / `findings`) avoided desk and Agent Safety noun-swaps.
- Dual score (FB-aware vs dropped-FB) made the paper claim testable without claiming a live PLC verifier.
- Trigger synthesis as a first-class page matched the buyer story better than burying it in findings only.

## What to watch
- Soft sims must keep honesty fences loud — OT buyers will assume “formal verification” means certified tooling.
- Bomb taxonomy labels must stay grounded in ladder patterns (timers, FB nesting, interlocks), not generic “alerts.”
- Goldens should stress nested FB + high retention so A clearly beats B on catch rate.
