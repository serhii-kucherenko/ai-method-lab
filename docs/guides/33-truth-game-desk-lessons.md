# Truth Game Desk — lessons

What we learned climbing a game-theoretic multi-agent truth desk from paper 2607.08403 through sustain.

## Domain before costume

The unique claim is **game-theoretic multi-agent truth plans** (structured challenge, payoff scoring, multi-agent game awareness) versus single-agent unchecked answers, flat majority vote without game structure, and confidence-only filters. If the UI still talks about multimodal consult benches, chest X-ray pathways, or dual-approver boards after a clone, the product is isomorphic theater — scrub it before sustain.

## Dual-impl is the honesty engine

Keep two scorers that must agree on ≥25–30 goldens. Renaming strategy labels without regenerating fixtures and checking both paths is how silent drift ships.
