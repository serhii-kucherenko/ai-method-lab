# Lessons — Optical Stack Studio

## What worked

- **Domain-first IA** — `/briefs` `/materials` `/thicknesses` `/stacks` `/spectra` `/compare` reads as a coatings inverse-design workflow, not a noun-swapped desk or thorax localize clone.
- **Dual score with an honest gap** — open-vocabulary discrete+continuous (A) vs catalog-only (B) makes the buyer claim falsifiable in goldens and on `/compare`.
- **Landing sells the coatings outcome** — free-form coating goals to multilayer stacks, not a generic “lab desk.”

## What to avoid next time

- Branding as IrisFlow or claiming live fab / spectrometer hardware; keep the honesty fence early and visible.
- Copying medical exam/finding/lesion shells — optical coatings need briefs, materials, thicknesses, and spectra.
- Skipping live `next build` + `next start` smoke; API/unit green alone is not sustain.

## Patterns to reuse

- Dual-impl scoring (`score.ts` / `scoreB.ts`) with ~30 fixtures.
- In-memory store + bearer/webhook/export for comprehensive API bar.
- Tutor guide + `try.html` before finish email.
