## Paper in plain words

Predicting how strongly a molecule will act (structure–activity) often hits limits with classical single-kernel models. The paper describes a quantum multiple-kernel learning approach meant to ease those bottlenecks in early drug discovery scoring. No public authors’ code shipped with the paper.
