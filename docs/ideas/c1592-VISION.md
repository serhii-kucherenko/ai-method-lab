## Who

Trade compliance managers and outside customs counsel who need a **worst-case statutory ceiling** under 19 U.S.C. § 1592 before disclosure / petition strategy — not a dual-approver status board.

## Why

§ 1592(c) sets **different maximums** by culpability and by whether duties/taxes/fees were lost. Spreadsheets that always apply “2× duties” miss the no-loss % path and the domestic-value lesser-of. Counsel and CBP still own real cases (Kill A).
