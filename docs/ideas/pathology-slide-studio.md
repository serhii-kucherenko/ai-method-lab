# Pathology Slide Studio

**State:** sustain-green  
**Slug:** `pathology-slide-studio`  
**Paper:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.09526v1  
**Authors’ code:** https://github.com/WonderLandxD/ALICE  

## Buyer
Computational pathology teams adopting a foundation backbone who need vision, vision-language, and slide-level signals in one workflow — not patch-vision alone.

## Outcome
A studio to manage slide cohorts, run multi-signal embedding/eval profiles, inspect patch vs slide views, compare against vision-only baselines, and audit runs.

## Why this paper
Public code, general-purpose pathology foundation claim, distinct from prior studios.
