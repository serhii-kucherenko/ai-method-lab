## Build claim

Hard feasibility rules must coexist with soft personal preferences. Travel packing is the paper’s instance; the desk experiments with constrained personalization: seed a checklist under hard rules, score soft preferences, then select a feasible subset — versus a naive preference-only baseline that ignores constraints.

## Honesty

Workflow experiment inspired by the paper — not a claim to replace FlyEnJoy, the authors’ RLO checklist system, or commercial packing apps.
