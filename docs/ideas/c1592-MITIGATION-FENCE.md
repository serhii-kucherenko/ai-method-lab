# c1592 — mitigated-guidelines honesty fence

**v0 must not claim** CBP mitigated / mitigation guideline starting points or final assessed amounts after aggravating/mitigating factors.

## Why

§ 1592(c) states **maximums**. Practitioners negotiate below the max. Encoding “guideline midpoints” without a dual-checked external table would be costume accuracy.

## Allowed

- Label outputs as **statutory maximum** / forecast ceiling.
- Reject cheats that ignore the domestic-value lesser-of when it binds.

## Forbidden

- “This is what CBP will assess.”
- Silent substitution of mitigated amounts for statutory max.
