# Disease Drug Studio

**State:** building  
**Slug:** `disease-drug-studio`  
**Paper:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08404v1  
**Authors’ code:** https://github.com/alimotahharynia/DrugGen-2  

## Buyer
Medicinal chemistry / early discovery teams who generate de novo candidates and need **disease context** in the generation loop — not generic SMILES toys.

## Outcome
A studio to manage disease programs, generate candidates with disease-aware conditioning, rank/filter, compare against disease-blind baselines, and audit generation runs.

## Why this paper
Public code, clear AI-assisted discovery claim, distinct from Graph RAG Studio.
