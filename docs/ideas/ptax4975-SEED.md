## One-line itch

Plan sponsors model a flat “excise hit,” but IRC **§ 4975** taxes **15% of the amount involved per year (or part year) in the taxable period**, then **100%** if uncorrected — and “amount involved” is a defined greater-of FMV rule.

## Primary pointers

- 26 U.S.C. § 4975
- Treas. regs / amount-involved: see `ptax4975-STATUTE-CITATIONS.md` (§ 4975(f)(4); Temp. § 141.4975-13; Rev. Rul. 2006-38)

## Why $ stakes

Excise is a literal dollar tax schedule, not Free/Free chapter theater. Wrong period counting or amount-involved basis invents or hides multi-year exposure.
