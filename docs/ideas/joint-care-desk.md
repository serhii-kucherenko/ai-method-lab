## Paper in plain words

Musculoskeletal care (bones, joints, soft tissue) unfolds over months. Clinicians need continuous, stage-aware decisions from admission through rehabilitation, but patient evidence is fragmented across hospital systems and external guidelines. The paper presents OrthoPilot: an evidence-grounded agent that retrieves hospital data and external medical knowledge, then produces traceable pathway recommendations. No public authors’ code shipped with the paper.

## Unique claim (dual-gate — not isomorphic)

**Dual-evidence pathway plans** (in-hospital evidence + external knowledge, missing-evidence acquisition, stage-aware pathway) vs **naive single-shot** answers that ignore missing evidence and stage transitions (parametric-memory-only / stage-blind). Soft desk simulation — not OrthoPilot / CHEESE / OrthoBench as a brand.

## Build claim
