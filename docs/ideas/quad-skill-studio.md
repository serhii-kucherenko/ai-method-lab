# Quad Skill Studio

**State:** ready_to_build  
**Slug:** `quad-skill-studio`  
**Paper:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13579v1  
**Authors’ code:** none  

## Buyer
Robotics teams training quadrupeds for wild terrain who need multi-skill locomotion with smooth gait transitions — not single-gait policies that stall on terrain changes.

## Outcome
A studio to register robots, map terrains, curate motor skills, manage trajectory/RL training datasets, review gait transitions, and compare against single-skill baselines.

## Why this paper
Clear robotics software claim (APT-RL multi-skill + perception). Distinct from prior studios. No public authors’ code — Method Lab product inspired by the pattern, not a rebrand of APT-RL.
