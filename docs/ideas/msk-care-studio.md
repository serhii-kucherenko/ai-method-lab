# MSK Care Studio

**State:** ready_to_build  
**Slug:** `msk-care-studio`  
**Paper:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.12527v1  
**Authors’ code:** none  

## Buyer
Musculoskeletal care teams who need evidence-grounded decisions across admission → rehab — not ad-hoc LLM suggestions disconnected from hospital state and medical knowledge.

## Outcome
A studio to track patient episodes, link hospital state streams, ground decisions in external knowledge, manage care pathways, and compare against ungrounded LLM baselines.

## Why this paper
Clear clinical AI software claim (OrthoPilot-style continuous MSK management). Stronger product story than survey/governance papers. No public authors’ code — Method Lab product inspired by the pattern, not a rebrand of OrthoPilot.
