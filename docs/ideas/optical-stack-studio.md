# Optical Stack Studio

**State:** ready_to_build  
**Slug:** `optical-stack-studio`  
**Paper:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08392v1  
**Authors’ code:** none  

## Buyer
Optics / coatings engineers who need open-vocabulary inverse design of multilayer stacks (materials + thicknesses) — not fixed-catalog optimizers that can't take free-form specs.

## Outcome
A studio to capture open-vocabulary design briefs, propose material sequences, tune continuous thicknesses, review stack spectra, and compare against catalog-only baselines.

## Why this paper
Clear inverse-design software claim (discrete+continuous flow matching for coatings). Breaks the medical streak with a distinct industrial buyer. No public authors’ code — Method Lab product inspired by IrisFlow patterns, not a rebrand.
