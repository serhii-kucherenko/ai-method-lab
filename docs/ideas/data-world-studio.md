## Buyer
Teams building autonomous data science agents who waste cycles on trial-and-error execution and need outcome forecasts before they run expensive ops.

## Outcome
A studio to manage DS workspaces, catalog operations, forecast outcomes with a world-model plan, configure agents, review rollouts, and compare against trial-and-error baselines.
