## Build claim

Trip itineraries must stay feasible under hard time and place windows, then rank by soft desirability, then refine on-device without ever leaving the feasible set. Naive preference-only schedules can look appealing and still break opening hours or day budgets.

## Honesty

Workflow experiment inspired by the paper — not a claim to replace FlyEnJoy, PLA, or commercial trip planners.
