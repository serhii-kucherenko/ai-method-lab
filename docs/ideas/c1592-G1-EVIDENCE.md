# c1592 — G1 evidence (provisional)

## Named user

Trade compliance managers and outside customs counsel modeling **worst-case civil penalty exposure** under 19 U.S.C. § 1592 before disclosure / petition strategy.

## Frequency

Not daily. Triggered by CF-28/29 paths, audits, internal reasonable-care findings, or voluntary disclosure prep — **episode-driven**, high-dollar.

## Painful workaround today

Spreadsheets that apply a **single multiple** (often “2× duties”) without:

1. Switching to **% of dutiable value** when there is **no** duty loss
2. Applying **lesser of** domestic value vs N× loss
3. Capping **fraud** at domestic value (which can be *lower* than a gross-negligence 4× path)
